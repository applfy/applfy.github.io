Suggested Hotels

Welcome to the Maison Soquet - tucked away beneath glowing red lanterns on the edge of hip Pigalle, this luxurious boutique hotel occupies a Belle Époque building with a colorful, hedonistic history. Old World glamour is twinned with up-to-the-minute amenities such as free WiFi, and guests have access to an indulgent full-service spa, as well as a tranquil indoor pool and a steam room. An elegant bar offers a range of refreshments - Expedia.

Hôtel Da Vinci & Spa features a full-service spa and an indoor pool. The hotel offers a restaurant. A bar/lounge is on site where guests can unwind with a drink. Public areas are equipped with complimentary wireless Internet access. This Paris hotel also offers tour/ticket assistance, multilingual staff, and concierge services. For a surcharge, a roundtrip airport shuttle (available 24 hours) is offered to guests - Expedia.

Sightseeing

With its famous tapering cast iron tip, the Eiffel Tower is not just the symbol of Paris but of all of France. Erected by Gustave Eiffel to commemorate the centennial of the French Revolution in 1889, the 1,050-foot (320-meter) tower once held the title of the world's tallest structure - Viator.

The Louvre Museum is unquestionably one of the finest art galleries in the world. Home to hundreds of thousands of classic and modern masterpieces like the Mona Lisa, the Louvre is the icing on the cake of French culture and a testament to European art history – it’s not surprising that it’s also the most visited art gallery in the world - The Paris Pass.
